| Topic | Advice |
|-------|--------|

| **Currency** | ATMs are plentiful; carry some cash (₹500‑₹2 000 notes) for small stalls. Credit cards accepted in hotels, restaurants & malls. |
| **Weather** | Late July is monsoon season – expect brief showers, high humidity (≈30‑35 °C). Pack a lightweight rain jacket, waterproof shoes, and an umbrella. |
| **Health** | Drink bottled water only. Carry basic meds for stomach upset (common with street food). |
| **Dress code** | Modest clothing for temples (cover shoulders & knees). Comfortable shoes for walking on uneven pavements. |
| **Safety** | Kolkata is generally safe for tourists. Keep bags zipped, avoid isolated streets after dark, and use reputable rides (Uber/Ola). |
| **Connectivity** | Buy a prepaid **Jio** or **Airtel** SIM at the airport (≈₹300 for 5 GB data, 30‑day validity). |
| **Local etiquette** | Greet with “Namaste”. When visiting religious sites, remove shoes and avoid loud conversations. |
