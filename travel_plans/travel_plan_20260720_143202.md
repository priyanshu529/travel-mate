## 5️⃣ PRACTICAL TIPS FOR A SOLO TRAVELLER  

| Tip | Why it matters |
|-----|-----------------|
| **Book flights & hotels with free‑cancellation** | Gives flexibility if prices drop or plans change. |
| **Download local apps:** <br>• **Grab** (rides, food) <br>• **MyRapid** (LRT/MRT tickets) <br>• **Touch ‘n Go** e‑wallet | Saves time and often cheaper than cash. |
| **Carry a small amount of cash (₹5,000) + a Visa/Mastercard** | Most places accept cards, but night markets prefer cash. |
| **Dress modestly for mosques** (cover shoulders & knees) | Required for entry; you can keep a light scarf in your bag. |
| **Stay hydrated & use sunscreen** | Malaysia’s tropical heat can be intense, especially in Cameron Highlands and outdoor sites. |
| **Safety:** Keep your passport copy separate, lock valuables in the hotel safe, and avoid isolated areas after dark. |
| **Language:** English is widely spoken in tourist zones; a few Malay phrases (“Terima kasih” = thank you) go a long way. |
