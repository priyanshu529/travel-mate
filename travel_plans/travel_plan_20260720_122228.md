## 4️⃣ PRACTICAL TIPS TO STAY ON BUDGET  

| Tip | How it saves money |
|-----|--------------------|
| **Book flights & hotels together on aggregators (MakeMyTrip, Cleartrip)** – you often get “bundle discounts”. |
| **Travel off‑peak (May‑June or Sep‑Oct)** – flights drop 15‑20 % and hotels have “early‑bird” rates. |
| **Use public transport** – KL’s **LRT/MRT**, Penang’s **Rapid Bus**, and the **ETS** train are cheap and reliable. |
| **Eat like a local** – hawker centres (Nasi Lemak, Char‑kway‑teow, Satay) cost ₹150‑₹350 per meal. |
| **Free attractions** – KLCC Park, Batu Caves (outside), George Town street art, beaches. |
| **Buy a prepaid SIM (e.g., Digi or Celcom)** – ₹500 for 7 days data, cheaper than roaming. |
| **Travel insurance** – choose a plan that covers medical & trip cancellation; it’s a small cost for peace of mind. |
| **Currency exchange** – convert INR to MYR at the airport (≈ ₹18/MYR) or use a local ATM for better rates. |
| **Cash‑less payments** – most places accept **PayNow/GrabPay**; no need to carry large cash. |
