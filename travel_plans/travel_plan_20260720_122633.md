# 📅 Itinerary
## Day 1 – Arrival – Kuala Lumpur
- **Morning:** Land at KLIA2, Grab to Aloft KL Sentral, check‑in.  
- **Afternoon:** Lunch at Jalan Alor, stroll KLCC Park for first view of the Petronas Towers.  
- **Evening:** Dinner at Lot 10 Hutong, optional night‑cap at Heli Lounge Bar (city skyline).

## Day 2 – KL City Highlights
- **Morning:** Breakfast at hotel, LRT to Batu Caves (free entry, optional RM 5 rope‑climb).  
- **Afternoon:** Visit Merdeka Square, Sultan Abdul Samad Building, National Mosque; lunch at Nasi Kandar Pelita.  
- **Evening:** Explore Bukit Bintang, sky‑deck (RM 10), dinner on Jalan Alor.

## Day 3 – Genting Highlands
- **Morning:** Grab to Genting Bus Terminal (RM 15), Skyway cable car (RM 8 round‑trip).  
- **Mid‑day:** Check‑in at Mövenpick, lunch at Food Republic.  
- **Evening:** Genting SkyWorlds theme park (RM 130) and casino promenade (free entry).

## Day 4 – Historical Malacca
- **Morning:** ETS train to Melaka Sentral (RM 35), check‑in at The Rucksack Caratel.  
- **Afternoon:** Guided heritage walk (free, tip RM 10); lunch – Chicken Rice Ball at Hoi Kee.  
- **Evening:** Malacca River sunset cruise (RM 30), Jonker Street night market (cendol, pisang goreng).

## Day 5 – Penang – Culture & Food
- **Morning:** Bus to Penang (RM 55) or budget flight (RM 120); check‑in at Chulia Heritage Hotel.  
- **Afternoon:** Lunch at Gurney Drive Hawker Centre, explore George Town street art.  
- **Evening:** Dinner at New Lane Hawker (Char Koay Teow), stroll Esplanade.

## Day 6 – Penang – Nature & Relax
- **Morning:** Breakfast at Kafe Heng Huat, Penang Hill funicular (RM 30).  
- **Afternoon:** Lunch at Kebun Bunga, visit Kek Lok Si Temple (free, optional donation).  
- **Evening:** Sunset at Batu Ferringhi Beach, optional water‑sports (jet‑ski ≈ RM 80), night market for souvenirs.

## Day 7 – Return to Kuala Lumpur & Departure
- **Morning:** Fly/bus back to KL, store luggage at KL Sentral lockers (RM 5).  
- **Afternoon:** Last‑minute shopping at Central Market (budget RM 100), light lunch – Nasi Lemak Tanglin.  
- **Evening:** Grab to KLIA2 (RM 60), depart Malaysia.
