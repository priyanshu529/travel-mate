## 5️⃣ Practical Tips & Hacks  

| Tip | How it Saves Money / Improves Experience |
|-----|-------------------------------------------|
| **Book flights & hotels together on a single platform** (e.g., MakeMyTrip, Cleartrip) – you may snag a “flight+hotel” combo discount of up to 5 %. |
| **Use the KLIA Ekspres** for the first/last airport leg (₹1 300) – faster than a Grab and you can work or relax on board. |
| **Grab “Go‑Pay” promo codes** – first‑time users get ~₹200 off each ride. |
| **Buy a **MRT/LRT Unlimited Day Pass** (₹150) for days you’ll be hopping between stations a lot (Day 1 & Day 2). |
| **Eat where locals eat** – food courts, hawker centres, and night markets give meals for ₹150‑₹300. |
| **Free Wi‑Fi** at most malls, cafes & the hotel – avoid roaming charges. |
| **Carry a reusable water bottle** – tap water is safe in KL; you’ll save on bottled water. |
| **Currency:** Use a **multi‑currency travel card** (e.g., Niyo, Revolut) – 0 % foreign‑exchange fee vs. 3‑4 % at airport counters. |
| **Travel Insurance** – a basic plan (~₹800) is worth it for medical coverage. |
