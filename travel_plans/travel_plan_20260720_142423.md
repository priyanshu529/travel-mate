| Tip | How it Saves Money / Improves Experience |
|-----|--------------------------------------------|
| **Book flights & hotels together on a “flight‑hotel combo” site** (MakeMyTrip, Cleartrip) – you can shave 5‑10 % off the total. |
| **Use a prepaid travel card** (e.g., HDFC Travel Card) – lower forex fees if you plan any cross‑border spend (e.g., for the NRT fare you saw). |
| **Travel by scooter** for intra‑city hops (₹ 300‑₹ 400/day) – far cheaper than Uber for short distances. |
| **Eat where locals eat** – street stalls in **VV Puram Food Street** (₹ 100‑₹ 250 per meal) give authentic taste at a fraction of restaurant prices. |
| **Free Wi‑Fi zones** – many cafés and malls (e.g., **Starbucks**, **Cafe Coffee Day**) offer free internet; avoid buying a data‑roaming pack. |
| **Carry a reusable water bottle** – refill at hotel or public water points; you’ll save ~₹ 50 per day. |
| **Travel insurance** – a basic ₹ 500‑₹ 800 policy covers medical emergencies and lost luggage; worth the peace of mind. |
